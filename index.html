You don't need a like button!

LikedIt is a program that provide not only evaluating a program by just watching it for viewers, but also a reliable and detailed feedback for owners of videos. The program understands viewer’s like rate by processing his facial expressions, while he is watching the video, and does this in real time! This means we don't need any face recording. After the video is over, a detailed report about the viewer’s like rate and the scene he is most interest in is sent to the owner of the video. Also, users can find new videos which are recommended by the program based on the videos they like. For details take a look at our documents.
